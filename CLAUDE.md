## Architecture

The system integrates four disciplines with AI as the central layer:
- ML: Random Forest expense classifier (89% accuracy), K-means user segmentation (4 clusters), Financial Health Score (0-100 composite)
- Economics: Time value of money, inflation, risk-return tradeoff, behavioral economics
- Animation: 5 animated modules (Snowball, Building Blocks, Silent Thief, Roller Coaster, Bucket System)
- CSIT: React.js frontend, Flask backend, SQLite/PostgreSQL
